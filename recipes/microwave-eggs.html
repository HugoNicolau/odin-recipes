Lasagna Recipe

Description

Use your microwave to make light and fluffy scrambled eggs for a quick and easy breakfast to start your day. Follow the technique in this recipe for perfect results every time.

Ingredients

  • Egg

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a microwave-safe bowl or mug
  2. Mix well with milk and salt
  3. Heat the eggs in the microwave for about 30 seconds
  4. Remove and beat well
  5. Return to the microwave for another 30 seconds
  6. Repeat until eggs are fully cooked and scrambled
